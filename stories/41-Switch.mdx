# MLSwitch

[Ant Documentation](https://3x.ant.design/components/switch)

## Usage Guidelines
Guidelines taken from [uxmovement.com](https://uxmovement.com/buttons/when-to-use-a-switch-or-checkbox/)

When to use a switch 
* to control something that will have visible instantaneous change (like a light switch, e.g. switching from light to dark mode)

When to use a [checkbox](http://marklogic-ui-component-library.eng.marklogic.com/?path=/story/data-entry-mlcheckbox--basic) instead of a switch
* when the effect happens after pushing a 'save', 'submit' or other button for user to continue on journey (e.g. opting in for receiving emails when signing up for a new account)

## Text Guidelines
For labels, use:
* [Title Case](https://elementary.io/docs/human-interface-guidelines#capitalization) for phrases.
* [Sentence case](https://elementary.io/docs/human-interface-guidelines#capitalization) if the text is a complete sentence. 
* Precise terms to clearly convey what the switch toggles on or off.
