## Text Guidelines
For text describing a statistic, use:
* [Title Case](https://elementary.io/docs/human-interface-guidelines#capitalization) for phrases.
* [Sentence case](https://elementary.io/docs/human-interface-guidelines#capitalization) if the text is a complete sentence.
* Short, precise terms.
* Language that places the statistic in context.
